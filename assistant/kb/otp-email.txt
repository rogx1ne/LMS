Forgot-password OTP email troubleshooting

This LMS sends OTP using SMTP env vars loaded from .env (run.sh sources it).

1) Check .env values (copy from .env.example):
   - LMS_SMTP_HOST (e.g. smtp.gmail.com)
   - LMS_SMTP_PORT (587 typical for STARTTLS)
   - LMS_SMTP_USER
   - LMS_SMTP_PASS (app password, not your normal password)
   - LMS_SMTP_FROM (optional; defaults to LMS_SMTP_USER)

2) Gmail notes:
   - Use an App Password (Google Account → Security → App passwords).
   - Normal password often fails.

3) If email still fails:
   - Verify your network can reach the SMTP host/port
   - Check if your provider blocks SMTP or requires SSL settings
